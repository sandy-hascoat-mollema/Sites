Setting Up Your Intuit Credit Card Reader

Below are a few basic steps to get your Intuit Credit Card Reader set up.

Basic Directions

Step 1: Download the App. If using an Apple or Android device, go to iTunes or Android Marketplace to download. If using a BlackBerry device, please go to http://gopayment.intuit.com from your mobile phone & click the download link.

Step 2: Plug-In Credit Card Reader into audio jack.

Step 3: Launch the GoPayment app.

Step 4: Go to Settings and enable the credit card reader. If using an Apple device, enable the "Audio Swiper". If using an Android or BlackBerry device, enable the card reader by selecting "Choose Swiper". REMINDER: Since the device runs through the audio jack, please remember to set volume to MAXIMUM level when using.

Step 5: That's it. Start processing!

Troubleshooting

Basic reasons why the card reader might not be working:
Why does my device not work even when I maximize the volume on my phone?

  1. On some phone models, the Ringer volume is different from Headset volume so make sure the headset volume is maxed out.
  2. Make sure the magstripe is facing the right direction. If you are looking at the front of your swiper, you should also be looking at the magstripe.
  3. Make sure your speakerphone is turned off before swiping.
  4. If your phone has a protective sleeve, check to make sure the sleeve isn't preventing the device from being plugged in all the way.
  5. If you are using a BlackBerry device, make sure you press "Next" after swiping your card.

Not all devices are supported at this time, so please make sure the phone you are using is compatible. View list here.

Make sure you have downloaded the latest version of the app onto your phone.
