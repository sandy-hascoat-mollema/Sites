Easy mobile payments for home care businesses

Intuit GoPayment is the free credit card reader tailor-made for professionals who are always on-the-go and ready to grow. Just swipe their card, and you're paid. One account works for multiple users, so the payments will come to one place, no matter how many employees you have out making calls.

Homes don't have ATMs

You know how it is with homeowners: the lights are on, but nobody has cash. And the last thing you want to hear is, "The check is in the mail." Make it easy for them and convenient for you. Swipe their card, send a receipt right from your phone and get on to the next swimming pool, carpet, or poodle.

GoPayment can help grow your business

Being able to accept credit cards anywhere can pay off big. With GoPayment, customers can easily add services right then and there. And rather than sending an invoice later and having to track down a payment, get it done on the spot and increase your cash flow.
