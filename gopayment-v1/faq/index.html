What is Intuit GoPayment?

It's a free app and free credit card reader for smart phones and tablets that provides an easy, inexpensive way for anyone to get paid with a credit card.

It's convenient and fun to use, great for people who just want to accept a credit card payment now and then, and for anyone running a business. There are no set-up or monthly service fees at all, so it costs you nothing to keep it handy in case you ever need someone to pay you by card. If you ever want to sell that old TV on Craigslist, you know you'll want it.

Best of all, it comes from the personal and business finance experts at Intuit, the makers of TurboTax, Mint, and QuickBooks, so you know it's both reliable and safe.

How does it work?

You need just three things: Your mobile device (with a data plan), the free GoPayment mobile app, and the free GoPayment card reader.1

Simply apply for an Intuit GoPayment account and let us know which bank you want your money sent to. Approval is fast, often in as few as 15 minutes, and you'll get an immediate confirmation email so you can claim your free card reader. In the meantime, just install the free GoPayment app on your mobile device. When your card reader arrives just plug it into the audio jack on your mobile device, turn up the volume, and you're ready to get paid.

To accept a payment just log into the app, swipe the card (it can be either a credit card or a signature debit card), and enter the amount you want to get paid. That's it! There's no payment limit, card authorization is instantaneous, and an email or SMS receipt can be sent automatically.

How much do I pay? Do I have to sign a contract?

Not much, and there's no contract — here are the details.

First of all, getting it is simple and FREE: there are no setup or signup fees, no annual or monthly fees, no minimums, no transaction limits, no contract, and no cancellation fees.

If you never use it, you never pay a thing.

When you do swipe a card for payment, here's what it usually costs: 2.7% of the transaction. If you enter the card data in manually instead of swiping it, the percentage changes to 3.7%.

That's what most of your transactions will probably cost. The exceptions are for transactions the credit card industry calls “non-qualified”: those that don't meet specific requirements of the Visa/MasterCard/Discover network. American Express and corporate cards are examples of those (show me the fine print).
  • The 2.7% card-swiped rate applies to qualified swiped Visa/MC/Discover network transactions that are electronically authorized.
  • The key-entered rate will be charged on all qualified manually keyed Visa/MC/Discover Network transactions that have AVS.
  • Visa/MC/Discover Network transactions that do not meet those requirements, including corporate cards, foreign cards, and transactions that do not meet Visa/MC/Discover Network requirements for the best interchange program, will be charged a "Non-Qualified Rate" of 3.7%.
  • American Express and JCB may have different discount rates and transaction fees.

Do you collect a lot of card payments? If so, we have a special pricing plan for you.

How do I get my money?

The money from your transaction is generally deposited into your bank within two to three days of the transaction. Just make sure you set up your bank account to accept credits and debits by electronic wire transfer (EFT).

I have an established business. Is this right for me?

Absolutely. GoPayment works well with QuickBooks2  so you can easily track payments and customer activity. You can even have fifty users on a single account, so that you can track all your businesses transactions in one place.

In addition, GoPayment also has a special pricing plan that’s perfect for businesses: in exchange for a $12.95 monthly fee, swiping a card is only 1.7% (the percentage changes to 2.7% if you enter the card data in manually).

How do you protect my transactions?

Intuit makes TurboTax, QuickBooks, Quicken, and Mint, so you know we take the protection of customer financial information VERY seriously. Among the ways we safeguard your transactions: the card reader encrypts the data it collects, sensitive card information is never stored on your phone, and GoPayment adheres to strict online banking industry standards. Plus, each user has a unique login and password, so if you lose your phone no one can even access your app.

What if I need help?

If you ever need help with GoPayment, or just have questions about it, live phone support is available 24 hours a day, seven days a week.
