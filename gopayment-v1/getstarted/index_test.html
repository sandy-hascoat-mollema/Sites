Follow these 4 simple steps to get started

Step 1:


Download the App

Download the app from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store for Android.


  See Compatible Devices

Step 2:


Log into the GoPayment App

Login into GoPayment App using the login
that you created during the sign-up process

Forgot your login or password?

Step 3:


Plug in the Card Reader

Plug in your card reader and turn up the volume.

Still waiting on your card reader? You can still accept credit cards, see below. Haven't signed up? Click here to get a reader.

Final Step:


Swipe. Cha-Ching!

Yo are now ready to get paid!

Just swipe your card when indicated to accept your first card payment.

Did you know you can also take credit cards without the reader?



If you dont have your card reader yet, you can still get paid. Just key in the credit card information*. Your card reader arrives in about 7 days.
