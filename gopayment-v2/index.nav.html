Mobile payment processing made simple with GoPayment
To stay competitive, a small business should take advantage of mobile payment processing to keep payments coming in as fast as possible. When customers are ready to buy, you need to be ready to process their payments, on the spot. Mobile payment processing from GoPayment makes that possible. By processing mobile phone payments, and processing mobile payments via the web, you gain the ability to apply your payments to your bottom line more quickly, making your business more efficient and improving your cash flow.

Processing mobile payments is convenient for your customers
GoPayment's mobile payment system is flexible, affordable, and fast, for both you and your customers. You can process any major credit cards quickly, right there in front of your customers, and their cards never have to leave their sight. Mobile payments processing is secure, too: your customers' card information is not stored on your mobile device. You can really make it easy for customers to do business with you by processing mobile phone payments right on the spot with the mobile payment system of GoPayment. Just log in to GoPayment, select "Charge," enter the amount or swipe a card, click "Charge," and in seconds you have an electronic confirmation on your mobile phone. Email or text a receipt to your customer, and you're done: you've processed a mobile phone payment and given a customer peace of mind at the same time.

Why use the mobile payment processing system of GoPayment?
A successful business should make sure it does everything possible to make it easy for its customers to make purchases; these days that means processing mobile payments, including mobile phone payments and cell phone payments. So a good mobile payments system is critical. There are other mobile payment systems out there, but only one GoPayment. Processing mobile payments quickly and efficiently requires advanced expertise in managing money. GoPayment's mobile payment system is brought to you by Intuit, the makers of TurboTax and QuickBooks, among other trusted products, so you know GoPayment will process mobile phone payments, cell phone payments, and other customer payments quickly and securely. And you can save even more time, and avoid costly errors, by processing mobile phone payments, cell phone payments, and other GoPayment transactions directly into your QuickBooks financial software.
